Do you know what to do in Japan? Don't be satisfied by only going to the famous touristic places. There are a lot of hot events in Japan! As there're four seasons, winter, spring, summar, autumn, there're traditional seasonal events like Hatsumode, Hanami, Matsuri, Hanabi, and also people have other events like festival, exivision, nature tour etc.. Why not to join these events and find out the real core Japanese culture? You can find an events information on this site. Just tell us which event you are interested in, we can arrange for you a group of poeple, by your request, who come with you to the event and enjoy together it so you don't need to worry about any local or language matter.

The main concept of this project is, 1; Let you know hot events in Japan and try real Japanese culture, 2; To help you to join the event without any problem, collect people who can came with you as a guide and a companion. How to get the service: 1; you choose the events and open your requrement - what you need, and what kind of guide you want - in this site. 2; we collect volanteers and once the conditions meet, we organaize the group and asist you to enjoy the event with them!
